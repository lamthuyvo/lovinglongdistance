About

In the absence of physical closeness, how do you build a narrative and sustain the love that you have with someone else through just words or other snippets of communication?

Loving Long-Distance is a project that explores these communications through a video series and an app.

Follow @lovingLD

The Videos

We want to produce a video web series to tell the stories of two individuals and their attempts to bridge physical distances with written and verbal accounts. The series will start with the story a Vietnamese couple in the 1970s and their 9-year-long letter exchange between war-torn Saigon, Vietnam, and Hannover, Germany.

We're hoping to tell more stories like this — like those of a deported mother and her child, or of the professional couple that is pursuing exciting careers in different places. We would love to hear about your story. Please feel free to get in touch with us: wearelovinglongdistance [at] gmail [dot] com

The App

We will create an app that helps you revisit the stories and memories you shared with a loved one via emails, chat messages, photos and Facebook likes through an interactive timeline.

If you would like to give us feedback in how to make an app that suits your needs, please feel free to fill in the form below. You can also only fill out your contact information and submit the form if you'd like to stay in touch about our project.
